Arithmetic Operators

Types of Arithmetic Operators & Examples

1. Addition (+)

a = 5
b = 3

print(a + b)   # 8

2. Subtraction (-)

print(5 - 3)   # 2

3. Multiplication (*)

print(5 * 3)   # 15

4. Division (/)

print(5 / 2)   # 2.5

5. Modulus (%)

print(5 % 2)   # 1

6. Exponentiation (**)

print(2 ** 3)   # 8

7. Floor Division (//)

print(5 // 2)   # 2
